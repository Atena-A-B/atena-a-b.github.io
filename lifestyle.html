Hobbies & Lifestyle

Life beyond the ward — the things that inspire, energise, and bring me joy.

My World Outside Work

Living a Balanced Life

Beyond nursing, I enjoy living a balanced and meaningful life. I love exploring new places, taking photos, staying active, and spending peaceful moments with the people I care about. A full life outside work makes me a better nurse inside it.

Travel & Adventure

I believe travel is one of the best forms of education. Every new destination teaches me something about the world and about myself. Whether it's a quiet mountain retreat or a vibrant city market, I embrace every experience with an open heart.

I especially love discovering local cuisines, photographing landscapes, and meeting people from different cultures — it reminds me of the beautiful diversity in the patients I care for.

📷

Photography

Photography is my way of pausing time. I love capturing the light of early mornings, the textures of nature, and the unguarded smiles of the people I love. My camera is rarely far from my bag.

🧘

Yoga & Fitness

Nursing is physically and emotionally demanding — yoga and regular exercise help me reset and stay strong. Morning stretches, evening walks, and weekend runs are all part of my routine.

📚

Reading

From literary fiction to nursing journals to self-development books — I love reading. A good book is my favourite companion on a quiet afternoon or a long flight.

🍳

Cooking & Food

I find cooking therapeutic. Experimenting with flavours, preparing meals for friends, or simply making a beautiful breakfast — food is a language of love for me.

My Routine

A Day in My Life

🌅 Morning

Start the Day with Intention

I begin most mornings with a glass of water, a short yoga session or stretch, and a quiet moment with my coffee before the day begins. Mornings feel sacred to me.

🏥 At Work

Fully Present on the Ward

During my shifts, I am completely dedicated to my patients and colleagues. I find genuine meaning in every interaction — from a reassuring conversation to a complex clinical task.

🌙 Evening

Rest, Reflect, Recharge

After a shift, I wind down with a walk, a good meal, some music, or a book. I believe proper rest is as important as hard work — you can't pour from an empty cup.

🌸 Weekends

Joy, People, and Adventure

Weekends are for the people and places I love — brunch with friends, a day trip somewhere new, farmers markets, and always a little time spent just being still.
