My Passion

Why I Chose Nursing

Nursing was never just a career choice for me — it was a calling. From a young age, I was drawn to helping others and found fulfilment in being present during someone's most difficult moments. Nursing gave me a way to translate that compassion into action every single day.

I believe that healthcare is fundamentally human. Beyond medications and procedures, patients need to feel safe, respected, and genuinely cared for. That belief drives everything I do at work.
