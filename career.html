Nursing Career

My professional path in nursing, shaped by responsibility, compassion, and a commitment to continual improvement.

My Calling

Why I Chose Nursing

Nursing became meaningful to me because it brought together the qualities I value most: compassion, human connection, discipline, and usefulness. I wanted a profession in which care was not abstract, but something visible in everyday action.

As a person with strong energy and a deep sense of responsibility, I found in nursing a place where empathy and structure can exist side by side. It is a profession that asks for both skill and heart, and that is exactly what drew me to it.

Clinical Experience

The main dimensions of my work and growth as a nurse in the surgical section.

🏥

Surgery Section Nurse

I currently work in the surgery section of Velayat Hospital in Qazvin, where I contribute to the daily care, monitoring, and support of patients during demanding stages of treatment and recovery.

💊

Patient Monitoring and Safety

My role requires attentive observation, medication accuracy, and careful response to clinical changes, always with patient safety as a central priority.

💬

Compassionate Communication

I believe technical care becomes more effective when it is accompanied by calm communication, emotional reassurance, and respect for the dignity of each patient and family.

🔬

Evidence-Informed Nursing

Alongside clinical practice, I value research and ongoing learning. I try to approach nursing as a profession that should remain thoughtful, updated, and grounded in evidence.
