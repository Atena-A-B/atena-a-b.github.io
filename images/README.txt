IMAGES FOLDER — Atena's Website
================================

Place all your photos in this folder.

REQUIRED FILES:
---------------
profile.jpg       → Your personal/profile photo (shown on Home + About pages)
                    Best size: 600x800px or similar portrait ratio.

GALLERY PHOTOS:
---------------
gallery1.jpg      → "A peaceful morning"        (lifestyle)
gallery2.jpg      → "My favourite travel memory" (travel)
gallery3.jpg      → "Nature and calmness"        (nature)
gallery4.jpg      → "A moment of gratitude"      (activities)
gallery5.jpg      → "Adventure awaits"           (travel)
gallery6.jpg      → "Simple joys"                (lifestyle)
gallery7.jpg      → "Golden hour"                (nature)
gallery8.jpg      → "Staying active"             (activities)
gallery9.jpg      → "A favourite corner"         (lifestyle)

TIPS:
-----
- Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 1MB per photo) for fast loading
- You can rename photos to anything you like — just update the src="" in gallery.html
- Do NOT upload: patient photos, hospital private areas, or anything confidential

To add more gallery photos, copy any gallery-item block in gallery.html
and update the src, alt, data-category, and caption text.
