Academic Journey

The education and milestones that shaped me as a nurse and a person.

My Learning Path

Education with Purpose

My academic journey has been more than just earning a degree — it has been a process of discovering who I am as a caregiver, developing critical thinking skills, and building the knowledge base that allows me to provide safe, evidence-based care every day.

Timeline

My Academic Milestones

2018 — 2019

High School — Early Interest in Healthcare

My fascination with biology and human health began in high school, where I volunteered at local community events and first realised my deep desire to care for others. Science subjects were my passion, and the seeds of a nursing career were planted.

2020

Started Bachelor of Nursing

I enrolled in my Bachelor of Nursing degree, beginning a new and exciting chapter. The first year introduced me to anatomy, physiology, and the foundational principles of nursing practice. Challenging but incredibly rewarding.

2021

First Clinical Placement

My first clinical placement in a general medical ward was a transformative experience. I worked alongside registered nurses, practised patient assessment, and began to understand what real-world nursing demanded — both technically and emotionally.

2022

Completed Advanced Clinical Placements

Further placements in emergency, aged care, and community health settings broadened my clinical skills. I completed a certificate in basic life support and attended workshops on mental health nursing and culturally safe care.

2023

Graduation — Bachelor of Nursing

I graduated with my Bachelor of Nursing. Walking across that stage was one of the proudest moments of my life. All the late nights, the hard placements, and the dedication had led to this moment. I was ready to step into the profession I loved.

2024 — Present

Working as a Registered Nurse

Now working as a registered nurse, I continue to apply my knowledge in clinical settings while completing ongoing professional development. Every shift brings new learning opportunities, and I embrace each one with enthusiasm.

Future 🌟

Postgraduate Studies

I plan to pursue postgraduate studies in a specialty area of nursing — potentially critical care or nursing education — to further expand my expertise and contribute to the nursing profession in a meaningful way.
