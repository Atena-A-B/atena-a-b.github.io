Academic Journey

The education, research, and professional milestones that have shaped my identity as a nurse.

My Learning Path

Education with Purpose

My academic path in nursing has been both rigorous and deeply meaningful. It has given me the scientific foundation, critical perspective, and ethical discipline that support my clinical work today. For me, education has never been separate from care; it is one of the ways care becomes more responsible, more thoughtful, and more effective.

Timeline

My Academic Milestones

2014 - 2018

Bachelor Degree in Nursing

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Nursing at Kerman University of Medical Sciences, Iran, in 2018. This stage gave me my first strong foundation in clinical knowledge, patient care, and professional responsibility.

2018

Undergraduate Graduation

Receiving my bachelor degree marked the formal beginning of my professional path in nursing. It was a proud academic milestone and an important transition from student learning to clinical commitment.

2018 - 2020

Master Degree in Nursing

I pursued my Master's degree in Nursing at Guilan University of Medical Sciences, Iran, and completed it in 2020. This period strengthened my analytical thinking, academic discipline, and engagement with evidence-based nursing.

2020 - Present

Clinical Practice at Velayat Hospital

Since completing my postgraduate studies, I have continued my professional journey as a nurse in the surgery section at Velayat Hospital in Qazvin, Iran, where academic preparation and practical care meet each day.

2025

Research Publication

I contributed to an ISI-indexed article titled Association between muscular strength and sleep quality in older adults undergoing hemodialysis: a cross-sectional study, published in International Urology and Nephrology.

Ongoing

Continuous Professional Growth

I continue to look for opportunities to improve my knowledge, strengthen my abilities, and grow as a clinician whose care is both compassionate and academically grounded.
