Who I Am

Hello, I'm Atena

I am a dedicated and compassionate nurse who believes that kindness, patience, and empathy are the most essential parts of healthcare. I chose nursing not just as a career, but as a calling — a way to make a real difference in people's lives during their most vulnerable moments.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy photography, travelling, reading, fitness, and spending quality time with family and friends. I believe in living a balanced, meaningful life — one that nourishes both the mind and the heart.

I am always growing — as a nurse, as a learner, and as a person. I find inspiration in nature, in the stories of patients, and in the simple everyday moments that remind us of what truly matters.

What Motivates Me

The Why Behind What I Do

My motivation is simple: I want to be the nurse I would want for my own family. Every patient I meet deserves dignity, respect, and genuine care. That belief keeps me going even on the hardest days.

Beyond the clinical side, I am motivated by continuous growth — learning new skills, meeting inspiring people, and never becoming too comfortable to stop improving. Life is too short and too beautiful to stay still.

"Caring with compassion, growing with purpose."
— Atena's personal motto
