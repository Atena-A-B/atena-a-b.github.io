Who I Am

Hello, I'm Atena

I was born in the first days of January 1996, during a cold season that my mother still remembers clearly. She often says that, despite the winter air, I arrived with warmth and brought that warmth into our family from the very beginning.

Some of my earliest family memories still define me. My mother would gently complain about my messy and chaotic corners at home, while my father would smile and encourage me by saying that I was always "bringing order out of chaos." Over time, that phrase became more than a family story. It became a quiet description of how I move through life and how I care for others in my work.

I am a high-energy woman and a committed nurse. Outside the hospital, I find joy in gym sessions, photography, taking selfies, being close to nature, playing classical guitar, and reading Persian literature and poetry. I believe that life becomes meaningful when we love nature deeply and return that love through the way we live.

Personality

A Bit More About My Character

🌸

Warm and Caring

I naturally care for people with tenderness and sincerity. Whether I am with a patient, a friend, or a family member, I try to create a sense of comfort and emotional safety.

⚡

High-Energy Spirit

I bring energy into the spaces I enter. That energy helps me stay active, curious, and hopeful, even when life and work become demanding.

🌿

Nature-Centred

Nature restores me. I feel most grounded when I am outdoors, taking photographs, breathing fresh air, and paying attention to beauty that asks for nothing except presence.

📚

Growth-Minded

I am always looking for opportunities to improve myself, expand my abilities, and refine my skills. Growth, to me, is both a personal responsibility and a form of respect for life.

What Motivates Me

The Why Behind What I Do

I am motivated by the wish to become a better version of myself, not only for my own future but also for the people whose lives are connected to mine. In my relationships, I value mutual improvement, shared maturity, and the feeling that two people can help one another grow with kindness.

I believe in karma, and I find real pleasure in offering mercy and forgiveness to others. Some of the happiest moments in my life are the simplest ones: long conversations, laughter, and cheerful time with the people I love.

"To bring warmth, to grow with purpose, and to create order out of chaos."
- A personal motto I carry with me
